What’s In the Lake

The shelves, tier by tier — what we keep, today. A companion to the medallion issue — #3 named the three layers; this one shows what actually sits in each. Every box is a real schema in the lake right now.

The front is a curated map — Bronze (catalog, circulation, OpenLibrary), Silver (cleaned & joined), Gold (ready-to-pull marts), and the Platform spine that logs and validates every load. The back is the complete live schema: all 17 notable views, every column, with its type — 219 columns in all, generated straight from the lake’s information_schema so it can’t drift.

The zine

  • ⤓ The leave-behind (prints double-sided on letter — curated map on the front, complete live schema on the back)
  • ⤓ Just the schema sheet (every view, every column, with types — one page)
  • ⤓ The micro-zine (one sheet, fold ×3 + 1 cut → an 8-page pocket zine)
